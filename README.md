🚀 Digital Healthcare for Migrant Workers

Objective:
Develop a secure, multilingual, mobile-first healthcare platform that enables migrant workers to access medical services, health records, and emergency care. The app must support biometric authentication, unique patient-doctor IDs, and offline access for environments with poor internet.

🔑 Core Features
Secure Biometric Login
Face recognition & fingerprint login for patients.
Biometric + PIN fallback option.
Data encrypted end-to-end (AES-256 or equivalent).

🙋Unique Identity Management
Patient Unique ID (PUID): Auto-generated portable ID across states/countries.
Doctor Unique ID (DUID): Verified via government/medical registry.
QR code / NFC scan for quick record access in clinics.

🌐Offline Functionality
Access saved health records, prescriptions, and guides without internet.
Syncs automatically when the device reconnects.
Offline emergency SOS with SMS fallback.
Telemedicine & Consultation
Secure video/audio calls with doctors.
AI-powered chatbot for basic diagnosis & symptom triage.
Prescription uploads & e-prescriptions.

🖥️Digital Health Records (EHR)
Cloud + offline cache system for portability.
Workers can share their health records securely with doctors.
Records updated with timestamp + digital signature.

🚑Emergency & Safety
SOS button → triggers emergency contacts/nearest hospital.
Location-based services to dispatch ambulance.
Health alerts (heatwaves, epidemics, vaccination drives).

🌐Awareness & Preventive Care
Multi-language audio-visual education.
Mental health counseling (anonymous).
Preventive healthcare reminders (checkups, vaccination).

🌏Trust & Privacy
End-to-end encryption for all data & communications.
No data sharing without explicit consent.
GDPR/HIPAA compliance for international standards.

📱 Design Requirements
Simple UX: Icon-driven, voice support for low-literacy users.
Multilingual: Support for worker’s native & local language.
Lightweight: Works on low-end Android devices with limited storage.
Offline-first: Core features usable without internet.
Scalable: Can integrate with NGOs, hospitals, and government health programs.

🎯 Impact Goal
Give migrant workers a portable, secure health identity (PUID).
Improve healthcare accessibility & trust through biometrics and offline safety.
Reduce medical neglect by connecting workers with verified doctors anywhere.
